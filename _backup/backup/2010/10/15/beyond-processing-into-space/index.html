Beyond Processing — Into Space

October 15, 2010 Sebastian Meier

In fall 2010, I gave a three week intensive lecture together with Jan-Erik Stange at the Interface Design department at the University for applied sciences Potsdam.

Throughout the three weeks we looked into using Processing for creating generative visuals that could be used for 3D projections. Therefore we created a projection framework that enabled the students to map simpel 2D visuals onto a 3D object. The framework was making use of OSC for synchronizing 4 projectors.
